Passing, Setting, and Hitting

About

Game played by two teams of six players each, in which an inflated ball is volleyed over a high net. Each team tries to make the ball touch the court within the opposing side's playing areas before it can be returned. A team is allowed to touch the ball three times before returning it.

My Work

Passing

A volleyball pass is the end of the opposing team's attack. Also, passing means the beginning of your team's volleyball attack.

↑

Setting

If a setter can track down any pass and set it where a hitter can spike effectively, it does not really matter how poor the initial pass was.

↑

Hitting

A volleyball attack is an offensive move made by a player to score a point by hitting the ball over the net and into the opponent's court.
